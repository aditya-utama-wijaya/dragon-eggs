# Privacy Policy for Dragon Eggs - Learn Chinese

**Last updated:** February 2, 2026

## Introduction

Dragon Eggs ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Local Storage Only

Dragon Eggs stores your learning progress **locally on your device only** using browser localStorage or mobile device storage. This includes:

- Lesson completion status
- Stars earned per lesson
- Dragon Eggs (in-app currency) collected
- Current progress on the lesson map

**Important:** This data never leaves your device. We do not transmit, store, or have access to your progress data on any servers.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or process:

- Personal information (name, email, phone number, address)
- Device identifiers
- Location data
- Usage analytics
- Contacts or photos
- Any other personal data

## Third-Party Services

Dragon Eggs does not integrate with any third-party services, including:

- No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No cloud storage services

## Children's Privacy

Dragon Eggs is designed to be safe for children of all ages. Because we do not collect any personal information, our app fully complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar international regulations.

Parents can safely allow their children to use Dragon Eggs knowing that:
- No personal information is collected
- No data is transmitted online
- No advertisements are shown
- No in-app purchases are required

## Data Security

Your learning progress is stored securely on your device using standard browser localStorage or iOS/Android secure storage mechanisms. The security of this data is managed by your device's operating system.

## Data Deletion

To delete all your data:

- **Web/PWA**: Clear your browser's cache and site data for Dragon Eggs
- **Android**: Settings > Apps > Dragon Eggs > Storage > Clear Data
- **iOS**: Delete and reinstall the app

## Changes to This Policy
